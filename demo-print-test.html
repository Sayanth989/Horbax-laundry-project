📋 How to print on your TVS RP 3160 Gold

  1. Connect the printer via USB to your computer
  2. Make sure the printer is powered ON and has paper loaded
  3. Click the "Print Demo Bill" button above
  4. In the browser print dialog:
    • Select TVS RP 3160 Gold as the printer
    • Set paper size to 80mm × continuous (or "Receipt 80mm")
    • Set margins to None or Minimum
    • Disable Headers & Footers
    • Uncheck "Print background graphics" (optional)
  5. Hit Print — your demo receipt will print! 🎉
